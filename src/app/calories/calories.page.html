Understanding Calories

Learn why taking the right amount of calories is essential for your health.

What are Calories?

Calories are the energy you get from the food you eat. They fuel your body to perform daily activities, exercise, and maintain vital functions.

 Importance of Perfect Intake

Consuming the right amount of calories helps maintain your ideal body weight, improves energy levels, and supports overall health. Too few or too many calories can lead to fatigue, weight changes, and health issues.

 Tips for Managing Calories
  • Track your daily calorie intake
  • Balance calories with physical activity
  • Include nutrient-rich foods
  • Avoid excessive processed sugars
